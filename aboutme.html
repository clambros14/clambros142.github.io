About Me!


Hello!

My name is Caitlin and I'm a 20-year-old undergraduate at Fordham University. I'm studying Film & Television with a double major in Journalism.
I was born and raised in Baltimore, MD but have always dreamed of moving to NYC. Along with this I always dreamed of being in the film industry, even attending a highly-competitive arts high school to heighten my abilities.
I currently work as the Assistant to the Technical Director of Fordham's Theatre program. In my spare time, I'm often creating and developing new film ideas and short projects. Additionally, I'm a contributing writer and have been published with the Fordham Observer.
And to get to know a little bit more about me... My Letterboxd top four films are Ferris Bueller's Day Off, Knives Out, Aftersun, and Perks of Being a Wallflower. Follow me here.
